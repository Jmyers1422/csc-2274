Traveling Tips

There are loads of differences between the US and Germany. Here are some tips you should keep in mind if you plan on traveling to Germany.

  • Public transportation is widely used in Germany so make sure you look for train and bus tickets.
  • As odd as it may seem, many public bathrooms require payment of some kind. The bathrooms that will ask for paymeny will usually be behing a turnstyle. Some also have someone posted outside asking for tips, these are optional.
  • Many places in germany don't accept card, so you should plan to use cash as the primary form of currency.
  • Germany runs on a different electrical system and you will need a step-down converter for your plugs. Be careful to check that your converter is capable of changing the elecrical frequency
