If you are planning to move to Germany long-term, you will need the appropriate visa. Generally called a residence visa, this is any visa that allows you to stay in Germany for longer than 90 days. The visas available to you depend on various factors such as education, experience in your field, or your purpose for staying. Here is a list of some of the more common visas:

  • Student Visa
  • Work Visa
  • EU Blue Card
  • Language Course Visa
  • Opportunity Card
