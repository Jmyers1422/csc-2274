If you are planning to move to Germany long-term, you will need the appropriate visa. Generally called a residence visa, this is any visa that allows you to stay in Germany for longer than 90 days. The visas available to you depend on various factors such as education, experience in your field, or your purpose for staying. Here is a list of some of the more common visas:

Student Visa

This visa is for those seeking to study at any of the german univerities.

Work Visa

This visa is for those who possess a university degree and seeking employment in that field.

Work Visa 2

This visa is for those who work in the trades.

EU Blue Card

This visa is for any citizen of the EU, it allows employment in any member country.

Language Course Visa

This is a short length visa for those taking german language courses in the country.

Opportunity Card

This visa is typically granted to graduates from german universities, or those who have a college degree.
